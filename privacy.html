Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

This policy applies to the Poppy Front Desk service, a managed AI call handling product operated by Poppy Marketing & Consulting. It explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

When our system handles calls on behalf of a home services client, we may collect:

  • Call recordings and transcripts
  • Caller phone numbers
  • Booking request details (name, reason for call, preferred appointment times)
  • Call metadata (date, time, duration, location called)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide the call handling service to our home services clients
  • Capture and relay booking requests to the appropriate office
  • Improve call quality and system accuracy
  • Generate performance reports for our clients (call volume, booking rates, trends)

3. How We Store Your Data

All data is stored in cloud-hosted infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. Access is restricted to authorized personnel and governed by role-based access controls. We do not store data on local devices or unprotected servers.

4. Who We Share Data With

We share data only with parties necessary to deliver the service:

  • Your home services company -- the client whose calls we handle receives call summaries, booking requests, and reports
  • Twilio -- our telephony provider that routes and records calls
  • Supabase -- our database provider for secure data storage
  • Anthropic -- the AI provider whose models power our call handling

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Retention

We retain call data for the duration of the service agreement with our home services client, plus 90 days after termination. After that period, all call recordings, transcripts, and associated personal data are permanently deleted.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Ask questions about how your data is used

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

7. AI Disclosure

Poppy Front Desk uses artificial intelligence to handle inbound phone calls. Callers are informed at the start of every call that they are speaking with an AI-powered assistant. No attempt is made to disguise the system as a human operator.
