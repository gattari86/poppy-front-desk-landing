What Poppy Front Desk Does

Your patients are already calling. Poppy makes sure those calls become appointments.

A booking-focused, voice-first system built for the realities of multi-location dental groups.

Answer Every Call

A warm, natural voice answers inbound calls at every location. Before hours, after hours, and during peak overflow.

Handle Common Questions

Hours, insurance, location details, services offered. Your patients get clear answers without waiting on hold.

Capture Booking Requests

When a patient wants to book, Grace captures their name, preferred time, and reason for visit, then notifies your staff in real time.

Notify Staff Instantly

Booking requests are sent to your team the moment they are captured. No delay. No missed follow-up.

Group-Level Visibility

Per-Location Configuration
